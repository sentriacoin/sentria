Sentria Roadmap: Powering the Renewable Energy Revolution:

Sentria is revolutionizing the renewable energy sector by harnessing the power of the Solana blockchain, offering lightning-fast, eco-friendly transactions while driving global sustainability efforts. With its innovative approach to tokenized renewable energy credits (RECs), Sentria empowers individuals and businesses to seamlessly contribute to a greener future while earning rewards. Join the movement that’s making clean energy accessible, transparent, and profitable for everyone!

With Sentria, individuals and businesses can seamlessly trade and track their contributions to sustainable energy initiatives, ensuring transparency, accessibility, and global collaboration in the fight against climate change. Built on an energy-efficient Proof of Stake (PoS)



Phase 1: Establishing the Foundation (Months 1–2)

1.1 Purpose Sentria is building a blockchain marketplace to tokenize renewable energy credits (RECs) for greater transparency and accessibility. Individuals and businesses can easily contribute to green energy initiatives.

1.2 Blockchain Development
Launch the Sentria platform on the Solana blockchain, leveraging its speed and energy efficiency. Integrate smart contracts for creating, trading, and redeeming RECs. Partner with REC certification bodies for validation of tokenized credits.

1.3 Branding & Outreach
Introduce the Sentria website and whitepaper to outline the project vision. Build a community through forums, social media, and partnerships with environmental NGOs. Begin onboarding renewable energy providers and early adopters.

Phase 2: MVP & Token Launch (150 Telegram members)

2.1 MVP Release
Launch a beta version of the Sentria marketplace to enable tokenized REC trading. Develop a mobile app for users to monitor carbon offset contributions.

2.2 Token Launch
Deploy the Sentria token on the Solana blockchain, benefiting from its low fees and scalability. List Sentria tokens on decentralized exchanges (DEXs) to ensure liquidity. Reserve tokens for staking rewards to incentivize network participation.

2.3 Pilot Programs
Partner with solar and wind farms to tokenize their RECs. Collaborate with corporations aiming to meet sustainability targets.

Phase 3: Scaling & Adoption (Year 0.5–2)

3.1 Strategic Partnerships
Work with enviromentalists and energy providers to integrate Sentria into national REC systems and expand adoption.

3.2 Expanding Utility
Introduce features like carbon credit trading and green project crowdfunding to broaden Sentria’s functionality.

3.3 Awareness Campaign
Launch the "Green Future" campaign to drive REC adoption globally, offering incentives for renewable energy installations like rooftop solar panels.

Phase 4: Global Integration (Year 3+)

4.1 Cross-Border Trading
Enable international REC trading using Sentria as a standardized exchange medium.

4.2 Advanced Features
Leverage AI-driven analytics to optimize energy consumption and REC utilization. Integrate IoT devices to track and tokenize renewable energy generation automatically.

4.3 Long-Term Sustainability
Create the Sentria Foundation to fund renewable energy projects globally and strive for a carbon-neutral network.
